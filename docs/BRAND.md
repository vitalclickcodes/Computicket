# Brand

## Personality

Trusted. Modern. Fast. Premium. Nigerian. Youthful but corporate.

The brand should feel like the default, dependable place to book — the way Booking.com feels for stays or Ticketmaster feels for arenas — but unmistakably Nigerian in tone, payments, and partnerships.

## Taglines

Primary candidates:

- Nigeria's All-in-One Ticketing Platform
- Book Events, Travel & Experiences in Seconds
- Your Trusted Ticket Marketplace
- Tickets, Travel & Entertainment — All in One Place
- Where Nigeria Books Experiences

## Voice

- **Confident, not loud** — short sentences, action verbs, no hype
- **Local, not parochial** — Nigerian context and pricing, global polish
- **Direct, not transactional** — talk to people, not user IDs

## Visual direction (to validate with design)

- Bold primary colour with one strong accent
- Generous whitespace, large type, photo-led hero sections
- Iconography that reads at 24px on a 5-inch phone
- Imagery of real Nigerian events, transport, and venues — not stock

## Naming

Product is **Computicket Nigeria**. Short form: **Computicket NG** or **CTNG** in internal contexts only. Avoid the short form in customer-facing copy.

## Don'ts

- No emojis in product copy
- No exclamation marks in transactional UI
- No "click here" — link the verb
- Don't promise availability we can't honour (e.g. "instant refunds" unless guaranteed)
